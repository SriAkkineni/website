From Humble Beginnings:

Team 617 began in 2001 as a research project for two students who were interested in F.I.R.S.T. Robotics. From there, we developed into a diverse team of dedicated members. We experienced our first bump in the road five years ago but our success has skyrocketed as the challenge of recognizing failure pushed us towards more improvement than ever. This year, we have over 50 members eager to build a robot even better than last year's rope-climbing 80 lb robot.


First Robotics

FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) Robotics is a yearly competition involving full size robots designed to complete challenges and objectives that change each year with the game. Each January, students gather for “kickoff” where we receive our challenge, and from there we begin building our robot with strict rules, limited resources, little money, and a six-week time restraint. At the end of the six weeks, we pack up our shop and travel across Virginia to compete against other teams from around the world.


Our Achievments

• In both 2002 and 2003 Team 617 won the Engineering Inspiration Award

• In 2009 our team won the Motorola Quality Award at the NASA/VCU Regional and the Virginia Regional

• In 2010 we were the regional finalists for the Virginia competition

• In 2011, we won 2nd place in the ILITE Robotics Off-Season Challenge

• In 2017, Enginerds finished 14th in Portsmouth, VA and 19th in Glen Allen, VA

• Our 2017 robot won the Judges award which is given to the team with “unique efforts, performance, or dynamics that merit recognition.”
