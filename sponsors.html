Sponsoring

Team 617 appreciates any kind of investment that you can provide for our team. Team 617 divides sponsorships into various categories, each with its own advertising benefits to the sponsor, gradually increasing with the amout of support given. Listed below are the five levels of financial aid for Highland Springs High School Robotics Team 617 and details on how to sponsor the team.

Levels of Sponsorship

Enginerd $5,000+

  1. Recieve a free Enginerd shirt for the 2018 year
  2. Large name and logo on our robot
  3. Large name and logo on our pit banner
  4. Shout out to your company with your logo on our Facebook
  5. Name and logo directly on our promotional items with "Enginerd" status(including the T-Shirt)
  6. Logo On our Website with a link as a proud Enginerd

Nerd $2,000-$4,999

  1. Name and logo on our pit banner
  2. Shout out to your company with your logo on our Facebook
  3. Name and logo directly on our promotional items(including the T-Shirt)
  4. Logo On our Website with a link as a member of the Enginerd family

Geek $1000-$1,999

  1. Shout out to your company with your logo on our Facebook
  2. Name and logo directly on our promotional items(including the T-Shirt)
  3. Logo On our Website with a link as a member of the Enginerd family

Techie $250-$999

  1. Name and logo directly on our promotional items(including the T-Shirt)
  2. Logo On our Website with a link as a member of the Enginerd family

Study Partner $100-$250

  1. Logo On our Website with a link as a member of the Enginerd family

How to Sponsor Us


If you are interested in sponsoring Team 617, please send inquiries to rbthompson@henrico.k12.va.us or send a letter to 15 South Oak Ave.
